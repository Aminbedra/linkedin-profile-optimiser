# Section Rewrites

Rewrite using these formulas. Always output rewritten text in clean, ready-to-paste blocks. Use marked placeholders for any fact you do not have, never invented detail.

## Headline (220 characters)

A headline is shopfront signage, not a name badge. It should answer "who do you help, and how" while carrying target keywords.

**Formula:** `[Role / keyword] helping [audience] [achieve outcome] | [proof or specialism] | [secondary keywords]`

**Example 1**
Before: `Marketing Manager`
After: `B2B Marketing Manager | Helping SaaS teams turn cold outreach into pipeline | LinkedIn, HubSpot, GTM`

**Example 2**
Before: `Software Engineer at a startup`
After: `Backend Engineer | Building reliable payment systems at scale | Go, Kubernetes, distributed systems`

## About / summary

Structure it like a short sales page, not a memoir. Use this five-beat shape, in first person:

1. **Hook** — one or two lines that name the reader's problem or your core promise.
2. **Credibility** — who you are and why you are worth reading.
3. **What you do and for whom** — concrete, keyword-aware.
4. **Proof** — results, names, numbers, or recognisable outcomes.
5. **Call to action** — how to start a conversation, and where.

Keep paragraphs short. White space is readable; walls of text are not. Put the most important line first, because LinkedIn truncates the About after a few lines on most views.

## Experience bullets

Replace duty lists with achievement bullets.

**Formula:** `[Strong action verb] + [what you did] + [quantified result or scope]`

**Example**
Before: `Responsible for managing email campaigns.`
After: `Rebuilt the email nurture programme, lifting marketing-qualified leads by [add %, e.g. 32%] in [timeframe].`

Lead each role with its strongest outcome. Where numbers are unknown, insert a placeholder prompting the user to supply one, since quantified results are the single biggest credibility lever.

## Skills

Choose skills the target role actually screens for, ordered by relevance. Pin the three most important, because the top three are the most visible and most searched. Remove padding skills that dilute the signal.
